My Best Projects This Year

    My first game project this academic year I completed was a game that would help portray what it is like to life with a modern controversial issue which happened to be about media addiction. I decided to call the game "Jaded", to reflect the inner mental conflict that is within the mindset of an addict.

    My second game project this academic year is called Sorcery and Steel: the Edge of Fate is a two-player fighting game with asymmetrical mechanics, set in a fantasy world on the brink of destruction. You’ll play as either a wizard bent on the world’s destruction, who is trying to use their extreme telekinetic power to call a meteor from the sky, or a near-unstoppable knight sworn to end the wizard before this can be done. This was a collaberated group project where I was the main designer for characters, assets and backgrounds.
